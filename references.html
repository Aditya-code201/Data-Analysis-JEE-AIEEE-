Data Sources & Methodology

Note on Data Compilation

The data presented in the chart is aggregated from numerous public sources published over the last 15 years. No single official document contains all of this historical data in one place. The figures are compiled from official press releases (from NTA, JoSAA, and individual IITs), reputable news articles (like The Times of India, The Indian Express), and major education portals (like Careers360, Shiksha) that track exam data year-over-year.

Small variations may exist depending on the source (e.g., "registered" vs. "appeared" candidates). The figures used are the most commonly reported and consistent numbers for each year.

Primary Source Types

This data is primarily sourced from official reports and press releases from the following bodies, as well as archived news reports from major publications.

Official NTA / JEE Main Portal

For JEE Main aspirant data (post-2013) and AIEEE data (pre-2013).

https://nta.ac.in/

Official JEE Advanced Portal

For JEE Advanced aspirant data and annual reports.

https://jeeadv.ac.in/

JoSAA / CSAB Portals

For official seat matrix data (total seats) released during counseling.

https://josaa.nic.in/

News & Education Portals

For historical data and year-on-year analysis reports.

Times of India (Education) Indian Express (Education) Careers360
